## Safety checks

- [ ] Does not make typological hypotheses sound proven.
- [ ] Does not introduce deterministic compatibility, dating, career, or military rules.
- [ ] Does not reduce medical, theological, public-figure, or military caveats.
- [ ] Does not route around the relevant specialist agent.
- [ ] Does not treat prior LLM output as primary evidence.
- [ ] Does not add uncited empirical/neuroscience/psychometric claims.

## Governance checks

- [ ] Reporting line remains correct.
- [ ] Instructions are not duplicated excessively across agents.

## Evidence checks

- [ ] Evidence labels are appropriate.
