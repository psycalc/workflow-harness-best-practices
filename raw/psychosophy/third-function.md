# Third Function

**Source:** https://sites.google.com/view/psychosophy/main-theory-official/functions/third-function  
**Author:** Alexander Yuryevich Afanasyev  
**Compiled by:** Psychosophy Library

---

## Abbreviated Characteristics

**"Ulcer", Process-oriented (Superprocess-oriented), Deficient, Dialogue-oriented, Flexible, Envious, Fearful, Doubtful, Compromising.**

## Key Properties

### 1. "Ulcer" Metaphor

**"We all have a failed side in us."**

In terms of its principles, the Third Function almost does not differ from the Second — it's also process-oriented. However, in its outward manifestations, the Third Function is strikingly different.

The Third is **not a strength, but a weakness, our "ulcer"**, a sore spot in the human psyche. It is a function that we ourselves consider vulnerable, flawed, underdeveloped, in need of constant strengthening.

### 2. Two-Sided Nature

The Third function is **two-sided**: the feeling of weakness is combined in it with the feeling of a huge, but not realized potential.

> "The Third is the chained Prometheus, a titan whose psychological chains make him weak, defenseless, vulnerable."

### 3. Vulnerability

**Vulnerability, heightened sensitivity** — are the main signs of the Third Function. Attacks on the Third are extremely painful, and the scars from them remain for almost a lifetime.

### 4. Prone to Exaggeration

The Third is prone to exaggeration, waiting for danger to happen, while in fact it rarely does.

- **3rd Logic** is afraid of accusations of incompetence
- **3rd Physics** — beatings
- **3rd Will** — humiliation
- **3rd Emotion** — tantrums

### 5. Fig Leaves

The Third function is envious of strong manifestations of the wounded aspect, so it starts to criticize this aspect, putting on a certain mask/fig leaf:

- 3rd Emotion → **irony**
- 3rd Logic → **skepticism**
- 3rd Physics → **prudery**
- 3rd Will → **foolishness and hypocrisy**

### 6. Compassion

The Third, like no other function, is able to **experience someone else's pain**, and feel it even more sharply than the object of empathy itself feels it.

### 7. Testing

Like the Second Function, the Third likes to test itself. If the test of the Third is passed successfully, then the **highest satisfaction**, unknown in other cases, comes.

The Third Function prefers to test itself in conditions that exclude direct combat and goes in these tests to the full limit, because it does not know the natural limit of its capabilities.

### 8. Sensitivity to Praise

**Successes, praises, awards in the Third Function are valued like no other** and are the subject of inescapable pride. The Third is more sensitive to flattery than any other function.
