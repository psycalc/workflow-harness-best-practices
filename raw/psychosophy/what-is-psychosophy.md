## Official Definition

From the official website (psychosophy.ru):

> "Psychosophy is a coherent system of views on human relations, written by Alexander Yuryevich Afanasyev in the philosophical-psychological treatise 'Syntax of Love. Typology of personality and forecast of relationships.'"

## Summary

**Psychosophy** is a typology theory that describes your psyche's values, sense of confidence, etc. towards four aspects of life: **Volition (Will), Emotions, Logics, and Physics** — how you interact with the world based on this attitude, and how it influences you and your interactions with others.

In total, there are **24 types** in the theory, plus an additional unnamed 25th type.

The original name of this typology was **"Psyche-Yoga" (PY)**, but later it was renamed to "Psychosophy".

## Interesting Facts

- Afanasyev, the creator of the system, self-typed as **LVFE** (1L, 2V, 3F, 4E)
- The official website is at psychosophy.ru
- The main book "Syntax of Love" is available on the website

## Related Typologies

- **Temporistics** — studies temporal aspects (Past, Present, Future, Eternity)
