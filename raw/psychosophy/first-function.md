## Abbreviated Characteristics

**"Hammer", Result-oriented, Overabundant, Monologue-oriented, Rigid, Rough, Cautious, Confident, Independent.**

## Key Properties

### 1. Strength and Gift

The first function is the **strongest side** of us and our **gift to ourselves**. It's the most important and most reliable tool for perceiving the surrounding world.

### 2. "Hammer" Metaphor

It's a **"hammer"**, equally suitable for *destruction* and for *creation*. However, this is a **blacksmith's hammer**, and not a jeweler's hammer — products created by it are **rough, simple and focused more on reliability than on beauty**.

### 3. Overabundance

The main feature of this function is its **OVERABUNDANCE**. If we feel that nature has endowed us with something not just enough, but even with some excess, then this is the First function.

### 4. Result-Oriented

The First function is oriented to **Results**, merciless in front of its opponents, does not tolerate compromise and demands absolute victory.

### 5. Rigid and Rough

The First function is **not flexible**. Therefore, strikes against it are very painful. Despite the self-confidence, it usually does not hurry to put itself to a test in a doubtful situation.

### 6. Selfishness

Selfishness, orientation to monologue, vulnerability, cruelty and roughness of the First make it the most significant and bright, but not the best side of human nature.
