# Intertype Relations in Socionics

Socionics considers sixteen types of information exchange (IE). Together they form the deep theoretical and practical section called "intertype relations."

## Types Classification

Intertype relations are divided into:

1. **Symmetric vs. Asymmetric**
   - Symmetric: duality, identity, mirror, parallel, quarter-duality, supervision
   - Asymmetric: ordering, revision, benefaction, infection

2. **By Comfort Level**
   - Very pleasant: duality, identity
   - Pleasant: activation, mirror, semidual, quasi-identity
   - Neutral: business, parallel, quarter-duality
   - Unpleasant: benefaction, supervision, illusionary relations
   - Very unpleasant: conflict, revision

3. **By Quadra**
   - Quadral: within the same quadra
   - Inter-quadra: between quadras

## Key Relations

### Duality (Optimal Compatibility)
Pattern: 1st mirrors to 5th, 2nd to 6th, etc.
- Each provides what the other unconsciously seeks
- Natural, energizing interaction
- No forcing on vulnerable functions

### Conflict (Most Challenging)
Pattern: 1st reflects into 4th of other
- One naturally gives exactly the type of information that for the second is weak and painful
- Constant pressure on vulnerable functions

### Activation
Pattern: 1st activates 6th
- One energizes the other's growth area
- Can feel like mentorship or chemistry

### Supervision
Pattern: 1st demands from 4th of other
- One demands what other struggles to give
- Can feel like constant criticism

### Identity
Same type - understands but may compete
