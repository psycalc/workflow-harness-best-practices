# Intertype Relations Ratings

Source: Type•volution (typevolution.com)

## Rating System

Star ratings for intertype relations, most significant for close friendships, family relations, and long-term romantic relationships.

**Interpretation:**
- For long-term romantic relationship: at least ★★★ recommended
- For fastest growth and personal fulfillment: ★★★★ or higher
- 66% = 3 stars (Good), 80%+ = 4 stars (Excellent)
