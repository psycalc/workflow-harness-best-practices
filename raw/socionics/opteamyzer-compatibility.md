# Opteamyzer - Group Compatibility with Intensity

Source: opteamyzer.com

## Approach

Opteamyzer uses physics, mathematics, and statistics to tackle typing inaccuracy and compatibility testing.

## Key Innovation: Intensity Concept

Introduced "intensity" across all dichotomies to eliminate need for subtypes.

### Intensity Examples (Introvert/Extrovert dichotomy)

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 5-6 | May appear more extroverted, interact freely, but info metabolism remains introverted |
| 1-3 | Pronounced introvert, deeply focused on internal world |

**Principle:** Same dichotomies can have varying intensities - one introvert may seem more extroverted than another, but info processing method remains introverted.

## Compatibility Analysis

1. **Receive Recommendations**: Actionable advice on optimizing team dynamics
2. **Analyze Compatibility**: Evaluates intertype relationships, identifies strengths/weaknesses
3. **Build Effective Teams**: Based on personality types of all members

## Benefits

- Customization capabilities for group compatibility
- Eliminates subtype complexity
- Preserves theoretical foundations
- Statistical approach to typing accuracy
