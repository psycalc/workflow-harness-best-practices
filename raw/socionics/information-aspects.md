# Information Aspects in Socionics

According to socionics theory, all existing information can be divided into eight aspects. Each type corresponds to one of four sources, each with characteristic properties.

## The Four Information Signs

Information comes from four types of sources: objects, events, structures of objects, and structures of events. These correspond to sensing, intuition, ethics, and logic.

| Sign | Focus | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Sensing** | Objects | Physical objects and their qualities |
| **Intuition** | Events | Changes, transitions, time flow |
| **Ethics** | Relations | Evaluations, reactions, attitudes |
| **Logic** | Structures | Laws, principles, cause-effect |

## The Eight Information Aspects

Each sign has "white" and "black" variants:

| Aspect | Name | Focus | Properties |
|--------|------|-------|------------|
| **BS** | White Sensing | Comfort, sensations | Intraverted, Irrational, Static |
| **CS** | Black Sensing | Power, influence, resources | Extraverted, Irrational, Static |
| **BI** | White Intuition | Tendencies, changes, probability | Intraverted, Irrational, Dynamic |
| **CI** | Black Intuition | Ideas, variants, possibilities | Extraverted, Irrational, Dynamic |
| **BL** | White Logic | Causes, patterns, structure | Intraverted, Rational, Static |
| **CL** | Black Logic | Consequences, technologies | Extraverted, Rational, Dynamic |
| **BE** | White Ethics | Relations, values | Intraverted, Rational, Dynamic |
| **CE** | Black Ethics | Reactions, behavior | Extraverted, Rational, Static |

## Aspect Descriptions

### White Sensing (BS)
Sensations, tastes, smells, well-being, tactile sensations.
Example: "This coffee has a rich aroma."

### Black Sensing (CS)
Power, influence, abilities, authority, status, capital and money, resources, strength, endurance.
Example: "He has the authority to make this decision."

### White Intuition (BI)
Tendencies, changes, and probability of events.
Example: "It seems like things are heading in a new direction."

### Black Intuition (CI)
Ideas, variants of events.
Example: "I have an idea for a new project."

### White Logic (BL)
Causes of phenomena, patterns, and structure of things.
Example: "This happens because of that fundamental principle."

### Black Logic (CL)
Consequences of phenomena, technologies, and skills.
Example: "If you do X, then Y will happen as a result."

### White Ethics (BE)
Relationships between people (friend/enemy, love/hate) and evaluation in principle (tasty/untasty, pleasant/unpleasant).
Example: "I have a good relationship with my neighbor."

### Black Ethics (CE)
Reactions to objects and events (laughter, crying, anger) and forms of behavior (giving gifts, greeting).
Example: "She always greets everyone warmly."

## Information Properties

Three pairs of properties define aspects:

1. **Introversion/Extraversion**: Information arises with the source (introverted) or from source interaction (extraverted)
2. **Rationality/Irrationality**: Processed through understanding (rational) or sensing (irrational)
3. **Dynamics/Statics**: Information about changing properties (dynamic) or stable abstract properties (static)
