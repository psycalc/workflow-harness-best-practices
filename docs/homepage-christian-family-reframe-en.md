# Homepage Christian Family Reframe

## Hero

**Build family on a strong foundation**

A strong family is built not only on feelings, but on shared values, fidelity, responsibility, forgiveness, and a supportive community. Christian faith gives this a deep foundation, while typologies can serve only as a supporting map of differences.

**Biblical grounding**

- **1 Cor 13:4–7** — love is patient, faithful, and enduring.
- **Col 3:12–14** — mature relationships require compassion, humility, patience, forgiveness, and love.
- **Eccles 4:9–12** — mutual support strengthens people; the threefold cord is not quickly broken.

## What is this?

**Claim:** This is a space at the intersection of Christian family formation, practical relationship discernment, and cautious compatibility tools.

**Biblical grounding**

- **Matt 7:24–25** — the stability of the house depends on its foundation.
- **Gen 2:24** — marriage is presented as a deep union.
- **Eph 4:2–3** — unity requires humility, patience, and effort to preserve peace.

## What we do not promise

- We do not promise marriage.
- We do not guarantee compatibility.
- We do not determine the will of God.
- We do not measure spiritual maturity.

**Biblical grounding**

- **Jas 4:13–15** — the future is not under human control.
- **Prov 16:9** — people plan, but outcomes remain under God’s providence.
- **1 Thess 5:21** — recommendations should be tested.
