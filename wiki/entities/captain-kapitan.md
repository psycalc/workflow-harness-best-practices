# Captain (Капитан)

Future is the strongest aspect of the Captain. From the point of view of Temporistics, the ability to influence the future lies in the feeling and vision of the dynamics of what's happening, i.e., "where everything is going."

## Characteristics

- Has a strong sense for changes in their life, their potential, possible consequences
- Can lay out a route to their desired future, make a plan of what needs to be done
- Future is the area of self-realization for the Captain
- When wanting to achieve something, first creates a **process** in their life
- Creates the route in their head—first step, then later steps, then what will be before the final
- Does not "go with the flow"; the first aspect is directive, controlling
- Feels enthusiastic when sensing positive changes
- Somewhat infantile—follows imagination, often not caring how realistic their goal is

## The Captain's Process

During the execution of a plan, the Captain needs to feel dynamics—that things are moving, not stalling, something is changing. Sensing positive changes, whether real or imagined/planned, the Captain feels a wave of enthusiasm. This emotion is one of the most important in their life.
