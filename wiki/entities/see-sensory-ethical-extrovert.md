# SEE — Sensory Ethical Extrovert (Napoleon / Gabin)

**Socionics type:** SEE (ESFp)  
**Quadra:** Delta  
**MBTI equivalent:** ESFP  
**Function Stack:** 1Se 2Fi 3Ne 4Ti 5Ni 6Si 7Fe 8Te

## Characteristics

SEE (Napoleon) is the politician and social climber. Known for social ambition, charm, and ability to navigate social hierarchies.

### Strengths
- Natural charm and social intelligence
- Good at reading people and situations
- Ambitious and driven to succeed
- Practical and realistic about social dynamics

### Challenges
- May appear calculating or manipulative
- Weak logic (Ti PoLR) means difficulty with analysis
- Can be inconsistent with principles
- May prioritize status over deeper values

### Famous Examples
- Napoleon Bonaparte
- Politicians, social climbers, entrepreneurs
