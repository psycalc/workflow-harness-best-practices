# Гуру (1 Вечность)

**Position:** 1st (Target/Целевой)  
**Aspect:** Eternity (Вечность)  
**Latent Process:** Deductive from meta-meaning — starts from big meaning, reads life through it

## Summary

The Guru creates their own ideology, their own faith, their own principles, and engages in spreading them.

For the Guru, eternity is the domain of self-realization, and **meaning** is the driving, motivating force of their existence.

In the sphere of meaning, the Guru is in position **"I plus, you minus"**: determining meaning is their task alone.

## Key Characteristics

- Capable of attributing meaning to everything around
- Cannot stop extracting meaning, moral — even without sufficient information
- Strong sense of calling, destiny
- Inclined to detached perception — looking at oneself from the outside
- Easily sees patterns and recurring events

## Latent Process

**Deductive from meta-meaning:** Starts from a big meaning, principle, frame, and reads life through it.

## Behavioral Markers

- After watching a boring movie, will draw conclusions
- Before sleep, summarizes the day
- Determines the meaning of their work themselves, even if position suggests otherwise

## Core Emotion

**Feeling of belonging/connectedness** — always arises when something gains meaning. The Guru is practically addicted to this feeling.

With prolonged lack of meaning — **melancholy** (negative emotion of eternity).

## Famous Examples

- **Эшли Уилкс** ("Gone with the Wind") — described as restrained, alienated
- **Герберт Уэллс** (ВБПН) — planned his time with pedantic "Scheme"
- Preachers, ideological leaders
- Founders of philosophical movements

## In Relationships

- May have strong views on relationship meaning and purpose
- Sees relationship as part of larger life meaning
- Can help partner find meaning in difficulties
- May impose meaning framework on resistant partner
