# LSE — Logical Sensory Extrovert (Stierlitz)

**Socionics type:** LSE (ESTj)  
**Quadra:** Delta  
**MBTI equivalent:** ESTJ  
**Function Stack:** 1Te 2Si 3Fe 4Ni 5Ti 6Ne 7Fi 8Se

---

## Function Profile

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism, organization |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Si | Comfort, physical sensations, practicality |
| **3rd (Role)** | Fe | Emotional expression, atmosphere |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Ni | Future projections, meaning |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Ti | Structural logic, analysis |
| **6th (Activation)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Fi | Values, warmth |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Se | Power, control |

---

## Characteristics

LSE (Stierlitz) is the administrator and organizer. Known for practical efficiency, leadership, and getting things done.

### Strengths
- Excellent organizational and management skills
- Practical and results-oriented
- Direct and clear communication
- Good at maintaining standards

### Challenges
- May be inflexible or dogmatic
- Weak intuition (Ni PoLR) means difficulty with long-term vision
- Can be seen as insensitive to people's feelings
- May dominate conversations and decisions

### Famous Examples
- Stierlitz (literary character)
- Administrators, managers, executives
