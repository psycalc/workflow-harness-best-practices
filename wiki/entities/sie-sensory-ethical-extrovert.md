# SIE — Sensory Ethical Extrovert (Zhago / Huxley's Huxley)

**Socionics type:** SIE (ESFp)  
**Quadra:** Gamma  
**MBTI equivalent:** ESFP  
**Function Stack:** 1Se 2Ne 3Ti 4Fi 5Ni 6Si 7Te 8Fe

---

## Function Profile

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Se | Power, influence, physical presence |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas, options |
| **3rd (Role)** | Ti | Structural logic, analysis |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Fi | Values, warmth, personal relations |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Ni | Future projections, meaning |
| **6th (Activation)** | Si | Comfort, physical sensations |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Fe | Emotional expression |

---

## Characteristics

SIE (Zhago) is the motivator and business-like actor. Known for adaptability, enthusiasm, and ability to seize opportunities.

### Strengths
- Adaptable and good at reading situations
- Natural ability to influence and persuade
- Enthusiastic and energetic
- Good at finding opportunities

### Challenges
- May be seen as manipulative or calculating
- Weak values (Fi PoLR) means difficulty with personal moral stands
- Can be inconsistent with principles
- May neglect deeper emotional connections

### Famous Examples
- Zhago (literary character)
- Salespeople, motivators, entrepreneurs
