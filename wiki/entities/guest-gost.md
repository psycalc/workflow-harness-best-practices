# Guest (Гость)

For the blind present, the current moment is something fleeting, ephemeral. The Present is not something important, so the Guest is usually ready to **sacrifice** their perception of the present to strengthen other aspects more important to them.

## Characteristics

- In rapidly changing circumstances and simply in unfamiliar surroundings, quickly loses their way
- Does not see their place, their role in what's happening
- Floats with the current, agreeing to whatever role circumstances push them into
- If the Guest gets too tangled in dynamically changing events, they prefer to wait until the present passes into the past

## Strategy

The Guest's strategy is aimed at **minimizing efforts** to determine their place. Among a huge variety of social and other spaces, the Guest chooses only those where they need to spend less effort for self-determination. Often the Guest is little concerned with their role in the family, at work, and chooses for self-determination, for example, some club of interests simply because in that space, a good place is given to them easily.

## Myths About the Guest

**Myth:** The Guest is unnoticeable among others - Hosts, Locals, Exiles.  
**Reality:** Not necessarily; they can be quite a bright person. However, they certainly won't stand out by the "place" or role they take for themselves.

**Myth:** The Guest is someone who "daydreams," "is absent from reality."  
**Reality:** Not necessarily. A person immersed in their experiences can enjoy them in the present without paying attention to the external.

## Famous Examples

- Zhenya (ВПБН): "The present is uninteresting to me because it's fleeting. A minute ago - that's already the past. In a minute - already the future. I have a feature of living either in the past or in the future and forgetting about 'now.'"
