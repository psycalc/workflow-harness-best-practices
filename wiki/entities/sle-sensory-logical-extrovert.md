# SLE — Sensory Logical Extrovert (Zhukov / Jack London)

**Socionics type:** SLE (ESTp)  
**Quadra:** Beta  
**MBTI equivalent:** ESTP  
**Function Stack:** 1Se 2Ti 3Fe 4Ni 5Si 6Ne 7Fi 8Te

---

## Function Profile

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Se | Power, control, physical influence |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Ti | Structural logic, consistency |
| **3rd (Role)** | Fe | Emotional expression, atmosphere |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Ni | Future projections, time |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Si | Comfort, physical sensations |
| **6th (Activation)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Fi | Values, warmth |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism |

---

## Characteristics

SLE (Zhukov) is the commander and leader. Known for bold action, taking initiative, and commanding situations.

### Strengths
- Natural leader with strong presence
- Quick to act and make decisions
- Good at reading the physical environment
- Direct and honest communication

### Challenges
- May rush into action without adequate planning
- Weak intuition (Ni PoLR) means difficulty with long-term vision
- Can be seen as aggressive or dominating
- May dismiss others' emotional needs

### Famous Examples
- Zhukov (Soviet Marshal)
- Jack London (writer)
- Bold leaders and commanders
