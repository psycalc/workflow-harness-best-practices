# ILE — Intuitive Logical Extrovert (Don Quixote)

**Socionics type:** ILE (ENTp)  
**Quadra:** Alpha  
**MBTI equivalent:** ENTP  
**Function Stack:** 1Ne 2Ti 3Fe 4Si 5Te 6Fi 7Ni 8Se

---

## Function Profile

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Ne | Intuition of possibilities, ideas, creativity |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Ti | Structural logic, consistency, classification |
| **3rd (Role)** | Fe | Emotional expression, atmosphere, influence |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Si | Comfort, physical sensations, wellbeing |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism, resources |
| **6th (Activation)** | Fi | Values, warmth, relations |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Ni | Future projections, time |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Se | Power, control, influence |

---

## Characteristics

ILE (Don Quixote) is the quintessential inventor and discoverer. Known for generating endless ideas and enthusiasm for new projects.

### Strengths
- Exceptional at generating creative ideas and possibilities
- Natural systems thinker who can see connections others miss
- Charismatic and enthusiastic about sharing ideas
- Adaptable and open to new experiences

### Challenges
- May struggle with follow-through on long-term projects
- Weak sensing function (Si PoLR) means discomfort with routine and physical self-care
- Can be seen as scattered or unreliable by others
- Difficulty accepting criticism of their ideas

### Famous Examples
- Don Quixote (literary character)
- Multiple fictional inventors and visionaries
