# Stowaway (Безбилетник)

When the future is in the painful, third aspect, such a person is called "Stowaway." Since the future in our perception is connected with directed movement, the term "stowaway" expresses the attitude toward the future as a place to which they have no rights.

## Characteristics

- Future is a sphere of fear and loneliness for the stowaway
- Feels weak and defenseless before the future
- Not the future depends on them—they depend on the future
- Perspectives and possibilities (main tools of the future) largely serve as a **criterion** of quality of life
- Lives under the weight of an internal judge constantly asking: "Where are you going?" "Are you doing enough?" "What is your contribution to the future?"

## The Stowaway's Strategy

The attempt to avoid the **criterion** makes the person with 3rd future a "stowaway." In their erroneous strategy, the stowaway tries to reduce their influence on the future to a minimum—they do nothing so as not to do something even worse. Let the future come on its own, and maybe it will be not bad.

## Recommendations for the Stowaway

1. Commit acts, concentrate on actions that change their life
2. Trust themselves
3. Communicate with people with 2nd future (Steersmen) who can help discuss plans
4. Communicate with elderly people satisfied with their lived life

## Famous Quotes

- "Often I just want to sit in the center of the city and watch people, especially the elderly. I want to understand what they're thinking, whether they're satisfied with their lived life, if they have something to remember and leave behind."
- "To be a little braver, a little more confident, daydream less and finally understand that my future won't be made for me by anyone else."
