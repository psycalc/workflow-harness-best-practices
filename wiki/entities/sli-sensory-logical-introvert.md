# SLI — Sensory Logical Introvert (Gaben / Dumas)

**Socionics type:** SLI (ISTp)  
**Quadra:** Delta  
**MBTI equivalent:** ISTP  
**Function Stack:** 1Si 2Te 3Fi 4Ne 5Ni 6Fe 7Ti 8Se

---

## Function Profile

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Si | Comfort, physical sensations, body |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism |
| **3rd (Role)** | Fi | Relations, values, boundaries |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Ni | Future projections, time |
| **6th (Activation)** | Fe | Emotional expression, atmosphere |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Ti | Structural logic, consistency |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Se | Power, control, influence |

---

## Characteristics

SLI (Dubrovina) is the artisan craftsman who values practical skills and physical comfort.

### Strengths
- Excellent practical skills and craftsmanship
- Good at understanding how things work physically
- Calm and grounded in everyday matters
- Quiet but warm in close relationships

### Challenges
- May seem reserved or indifferent to abstract ideas
- Weak intuition (Ne PoLR) means discomfort with many possibilities
- Can be seen as stubborn about their way of doing things
- May struggle to articulate feelings

### Famous Examples
- Dubrovina (literary character)
- Skilled craftspeople and engineers
