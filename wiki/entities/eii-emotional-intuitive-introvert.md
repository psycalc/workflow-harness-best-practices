# EII — Emotional Intuitive Introvert (Dostoevsky)

**Socionics type:** EII (INFj)  
**Quadra:** Delta  
**MBTI equivalent:** INFJ  
**Function Stack:** 1Fi 2Ne 3Se 4Ti 5Fe 6Ni 7Si 8Te

---

## Function Profile

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Fi | Values, warmth, moral conscience |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas, potential |
| **3rd (Role)** | Se | Power, control, physical presence |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Ti | Structural logic, analysis |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Fe | Emotional expression, atmosphere |
| **6th (Activation)** | Ni | Future projections, meaning |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Si | Comfort, physical sensations |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism |

---

## Characteristics

EII (Dostoevsky) is the humanist and moralist. Known for deep empathy, ethical concerns, and seeking meaning.

### Strengths
- Deep empathy and understanding of others
- Strong moral compass and ethical concerns
- Good at seeing deeper meanings and patterns
- Warm and supportive in relationships

### Challenges
- May be overly idealistic about human nature
- Weak logic (Ti PoLR) means difficulty with cold analysis
- Can be seen as moralistic or preachy
- May neglect practical business matters

### Famous Examples
- Dostoevsky (writer)
- Humanists, counselors, moralists
