# Passenger (Пассажир)

The Passenger usually doesn't understand how they can influence their future. According to the Passenger's philosophy, the future is something objective—it's not something they participate in, it's something that happens to them.

## Characteristics

- If asked how they see themselves in a year, they might give a detailed answer, but tomorrow the answer could be completely different
- The Passenger's future is too ethereal, unreal, and they cannot keep it in their head constantly
- Imagining a desired event in the future and determining steps for its implementation is a very difficult task
- When needing to make a decision regarding the future, they drag out time to bring the future closer to the present
- Often don't consider the future worthy of much thought
- Future is a resource, a bargaining chip—by not thinking about it, they free up energy for other aspects
