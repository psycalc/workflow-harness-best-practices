# Temporistics Overview

**Typology:** Temporistics  
**Level:** Strategic  
**Frame Type:** Temporal frame  
**Source:** temporistics.ru by Dr. Radut

## What is Temporistics?

Temporistics is a typology describing how a person relates to time: past, present, future, and eternity. It was created by Alexander Latyshev and Nika Sherman based on Nikolai Berdyaev's philosophy and Alexander Afanasyev's psychosophy model.

## The Four Aspects

| Aspect | Russian | Main Question | Negative Emotions | Positive Emotions |
|--------|---------|---------------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Past** | Прошлое | "Who am I?" | Sadness, pain | Satisfaction, feeling of power |
| **Present** | Настоящее | "Where is my place?" | Boredom, emptiness | Engagement, happiness |
| **Future** | Будущее | "Where am I going?" | Fear, despair | Hope, peace, inspiration |
| **Eternity** | Вечность | "Why am I?" | Melancholy, horror | Belonging, joy |

## The Four Positions

| Position | Name | Priority | Purpose | Berne Model |
|----------|------|----------|---------|--------------|
| 1st | Target (Целевой) | Strong (I) | Goal | I + You - |
| 2nd | Creative (Творческий) | Strong (II) | Tool | I + You + |
| 3rd | Painful (Болевой) | Weak (III) | Criterion | I - You - |
| 4th | Blind (Слепой) | Weak (IV) | Resource | I - You + |

## Type Structure

A person's type is written as 4 letters, e.g., **EPNF** = Eternity(1st) > Past(2nd) > Now/Present(3rd) > Future(4th)

**Notation:**
- **P** = Past (Прошлое)
- **N** = Now/Present (Настоящее)  
- **F** = Future (Будущее)
- **E** = Eternity (Вечность)

## Tetra Classification

Types are grouped into 6 tetras based on relationships between "face" (Present + Future) and "reverse" (Eternity + Past) worlds:

1. **Antipodes** (Н + П) — Game Master, Maestro, Player, Politician
2. **Guardians** (В + П) — Missionary, Standard-Bearer, Rescuer, Knight
3. **Old-Timers** (Н + В) — Star, Conqueror, Oracle, Theorist
4. **Conductors** (Б + В) — Pioneer, Colonist, Samurai, Ideologue
5. **Scouts** (Н + Б) — Sommelier, Grey Cardinal, Hacker, Scout
6. **Nomads** (П + Б) — Initiator, Robinson, Pathfinder, Tamada
