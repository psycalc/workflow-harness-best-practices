# Socionics: Tactical Level — Information Metabolism

**Level:** Tactical  
**Latent Process:** Information modeling (information metabolism)  
**Frame Type:** Information frame  
**Core Question:** "How do we perceive and communicate about situations?"

Socionics is a typology founded by Aušra Augustinavičiūtė, based on Carl Jung's work and extended with Model A theory. Unlike MBTI, Socionics describes 16 types using 8 cognitive functions (IM elements) arranged in a specific structure.

---

## Theoretical Foundation

### The Eight Information Elements

| Element | Name | Focus | Dichotomy |
|---------|------|-------|-----------|
| **Ti** | Structural Logic | Truth, structure, consistency | Rational, Introverted |
| **Te** | Decision Logic | Efficiency, pragmatism | Rational, Extraverted |
| **Fi** | Ethics of Relations | Warmth, closeness, values | Rational, Introverted |
| **Fe** | Ethics of Emotions | Expression, atmosphere | Rational, Extraverted |
| **Si** | Sensing Comfort | Comfort, body, wellbeing | Irrational, Introverted |
| **Se** | Sensing Will | Power, control, influence | Irrational, Extraverted |
| **Ni** | Intuition of Time | Future projections, meaning | Irrational, Introverted |
| **Ne** | Intuition of Ideas | Possibilities, creativity | Irrational, Extraverted |

## Intertype Relations

### Good Combinations

**Duality:** 1 provides 5, 2 activates 6 - Natural information exchange, low friction  
**Activation:** One energizes the other's growth area - mentorship or chemistry  
**Semi-Duality:** Partial function exchange - good but with some friction points

### Challenging Combinations

**Conflict:** Constant pressure on PoLR (4th function) - can feel like personal attacks  
**Supervision:** One demands what other struggles to give - can feel like constant criticism  
**Revision:** Asymmetric difficult relations - mutual dissatisfaction

### Quadral Values

Types within the same quadra share values and communicate more easily. Inter-quadra relations often involve value conflicts.
