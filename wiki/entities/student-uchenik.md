# Student (Ученик)

The Student is in some sense the antipode of the Guru and their complementary pair.

## Characteristics

- Knows their weakness and doesn't have enough strength and desire to create the eternal
- Not trying to transmit their worldview to others, to seek meaning, to realize their mission
- Their concerns are other, more pressing problems
- Ready to take what's nearby: accept someone else's ideology, participate in someone else's great cause, make their contribution to someone else's legacy
- Their aspect of eternity is half-blind, so the Student walks not along the most attractive path, but along the one that is better illuminated

## Attitude Toward Meaning

For the Student, questions of meaning may be interesting, but in the hierarchy of Temporistic values, they are in last place. The Student sacrifices the search for meaning to free up time and energy for other, more concrete, "closer to life" tasks.

## The Student's Blindness

The bearer of fourth eternity can easily fall under the influence of someone else's ideology and, having submitted, will not consider alternatives until completely disappointed.

The most important thing about the Student and eternity is that the Student does not consider answering the question "why" a personal task, neither in the context of their whole life nor in specific matters. They are happy to delegate this task to someone else and expect an answer from outside.
