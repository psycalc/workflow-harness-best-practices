# LII — Logical Intuitive Introvert (Robespierre / Analyst)

**Socionics type:** LII (INTj)  
**Quadra:** Delta  
**MBTI equivalent:** INTJ  
**Function Stack:** 1Ti 2Ne 3Se 4Fe 5Te 6Fi 7Ni 8Si

---

## Function Profile

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Ti | Structural logic, analysis, systems |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas, potential |
| **3rd (Role)** | Se | Power, control, physical presence |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Fe | Emotional expression, atmosphere |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism |
| **6th (Activation)** | Fi | Values, warmth, relations |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Ni | Future projections, meaning |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Si | Comfort, physical sensations |

---

## Characteristics

LII (Analyst) is the scientist and researcher. Known for analytical thinking, objectivity, and deep intellectual curiosity.

### Strengths
- Excellent analytical and logical capabilities
- Objective and impartial perspective
- Good at systems thinking
- Deep knowledge in specialized areas

### Challenges
- May appear cold or emotionally distant
- Weak ethics (Fe PoLR) means difficulty with emotional expression
- Can be overly critical or perfectionist
- May neglect practical everyday matters

### Famous Examples
- Analysts and researchers
- Scientists and system architects
