# Критик (3 Прошлое)

**Position:** 3rd (Painful/Болевой)  
**Aspect:** Past (Прошлое)  
**Latent Process:** Inductive — derives harsh conclusions from painful past episodes

## Summary

For the Critic, past is their **judge**. The question "Who am I?" is an acute, practically painful question that the Critic revisits with each new experience.

**Pain** is the main negative emotion of past — frequent companion of the Critic.

## Key Characteristics

- Past presses on the Critic with its immutability
- Cannot choose who they want to be — history is already written
- May spend long time chewing over a single mistake
- Easily breaks relations with people, even those they've become attached to
- Positive evaluation causes surprise and embarrassment

## Latent Process

**Inductive movement:** Painful past episodes → harsh conclusion about self

The Critic collects particular experiences, especially painful ones, and derives heavy conclusions about self.

## Behavioral Markers

- "Then I was silent. Then I stepped back again. Then I didn't push. Probably, I'm weak."
- Past events often processed with guilt, regret, self-criticism
- Tends to derive global conclusions from specific failures
- May appear to suppress emotions until overflow

## Coping Strategies

1. **Break with the past** — detach, forget
2. **Distract** — immerse in activity according to 1st aspect
3. **Self-abasement** — give yourself such a low rating that nothing external can hurt anymore

## Famous Examples

- **Франц Кафка** (3П) — before death, bequeathed to burn all manuscripts
- **Герберт Уэллс** (ВБПН) — uses eternity to look at himself objectively
- **Кролик** (Винни-Пух) — illustration by A. Latyshev

## In Relationships

- May carry significant past relationship wounds
- Interprets current situations through lens of past failures
- Needs partner who can help reframe without minimizing
- May push away partners who remind them of painful past
