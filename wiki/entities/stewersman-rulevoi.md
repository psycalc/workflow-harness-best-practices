# Steersman (Рулевой)

From the Steersman's position, the future looks less straightforward than for the Captain. The Steersman's future is in the creative aspect—it's not a goal, it's merely a tool for achieving it.

## Characteristics

- Does not build an image of their future
- Future is open to all diversity of life paths and routes
- Will change long-term plans without hesitation if interests change
- Can relatively easily survive a "turbid" life period
- Is a tactician, not a strategist
- Future demands nothing from the Steersman
- Subordinating their life to a long-term goal is much harder for them

## Strengths

- When the Steersman makes one step, they immediately see what the next step should be
- Their position is observant and expectant
- Well tracks changes in life that could affect their future and reacts to them
- Tends to trust the natural course of events
- Can help others not be afraid to choose a path and make decisions

## Famous Quote

As one Steersman (ПБНВ) said: "The best I can do is act so that tomorrow's me says 'thank you' to today's me."
