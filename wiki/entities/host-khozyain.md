# Host (Хозяин)

The person whose Present time is the area of self-realization is called the Host. Present is his target aspect, meaning all his aspirations are directed at this time.

*Key question of the Present: "Where is my place?"*

## Characteristics

- In any situation—whether a business meeting, a party with friends, or in line at a store—both the Host himself and the role he has chosen **will be noticeable**
- Non-verbally transmits "I am here" to those around him
- More capable than other types of involving others in his activities
- His motto: "Here and Now"
- A hedonist who knows how to enjoy the simple pleasures of life
- Often has an almost mystical love for nature
- Tends to drag out activities and processes
- Doesn't like rigid time limits

## Positive Emotions

Enjoyment, happiness in the moment, sensation of the present

## Negative Emotions

Discomfort when the present moment ends

## Internal Conflicts

1. **With 3rd Future**: Important to learn to leave the imaginary comfort of his "place" and make changes
2. **With 3rd Past**: Important not to ignore whether his personality matches his place
3. **With 3rd Eternity**: Important to enjoy the moment while also finding meaning in what he does

## Relationships

- For **Guest** (4th Present): The Host is a gift, as the Guest cannot engage on their own
- With **Exile** (3rd Present): Can make them feel unnecessary or out of place
- With **Local** (2nd Present): Neither captured by "his world" nor feeling like an outsider

## Famous Examples

**Characters:** Holden Caulfield (НПВБ), Bree Van de Kamp (НБПВ), Scarlett O'Hara (НБПВ)

**Individuals:** Stephen King (НПВБ)

**National Types:** English (НВПБ), Americans (НБПВ)
