# Local (Местный)

The Local's position allows its owner to be comfortable exactly where they are. If for the Host, the role is important for self-realization and he creates a role rather than choosing it, then the Local simply occupies one of the existing niches that best corresponds to their interests.

## Characteristics

- Does not cling to "their place," one role, or one line of behavior
- Sees the present in all its diversity, notices all current processes, and switches easily
- Quickly becomes "one of their own" when entering an unfamiliar group
- Desires companionship, partnership, and co-creation regarding what's happening in the moment
- Can immerse in what's happening without losing themselves
- Does not merge with the present; for them, it's just a tool
- Knows how to enjoy the moment, draw strength from nature
- Knows how to celebrate life, how to color it for others
- Does not drag out time; is fully adequate to the moment
- Feels "at home" in the chair they're sitting in and easily settles into new spaces

## Positive Emotions

Joy in the moment, comfort, happiness from simple pleasures

## Relationships

- When seeing **Exile** (3rd Present), wants and can involve them in the moment
- Understands that there is a place for everyone—important to just take it and not rush

## Famous Examples

- Maria (ПНВБ): "I feel like I'm 'in the flow of time'"
