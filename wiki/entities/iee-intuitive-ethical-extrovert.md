# IEE — Intuitive Ethical Extrovert (Huxley / the Journalist)

**Socionics type:** IEE (ENFp)  
**Quadra:** Delta  
**MBTI equivalent:** ENFP  
**Function Stack:** 1Ne 2Fi 3Te 4Si 5Ni 6Fe 7Ti 8Se

---

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas, potential |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Fi | Values, warmth, individual relations |
| **3rd (Role)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism, business |

| **6th (Activation)** | Fe | Emotional expression, atmosphere |

---

## Characteristics

IEE (Huxley) is the humanitarian and visionary. Known for seeing potential in people and inspiring positive change.

### Strengths
- Excellent at seeing potential in people and situations
- Inspiring and encouraging of others
- Warm and genuinely interested in people
- Good at mediating and finding compromises

### Challenges
- May spread themselves too thin across many interests
- Weak sensing (Si PoLR) means discomfort with physical routines
- Can be seen as unrealistic or impractical
- May avoid necessary confrontations

### Famous Examples
- Huxley (writer)
- Humanitarians, journalists, mediators
