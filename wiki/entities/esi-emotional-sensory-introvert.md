# ESI — Emotional Sensory Introvert (Dreiser)

**Socionics type:** ESI (ISFj)  
**Quadra:** Gamma  
**MBTI equivalent:** ISFJ  
**Function Stack:** 1Fi 2Si 3Ne 4Te 5Fe 6Ni 7Se 8Ti

## Characteristics

ESI (Dreiser) is the sentinel and protector. Known for loyalty, ethical principles, and protecting those they care about.

### Strengths
- Strong moral compass and ethical standards
- Loyal and protective of close ones
- Good at creating comfortable environments
- Attentive to others' needs

### Challenges
- May be rigid in their moral views
- Weak logic (Te PoLR) means difficulty with business efficiency
- Can be suspicious of new possibilities
- May struggle to adapt to changing situations

### Famous Examples
- Dreiser (writer)
- Guardians, protectors, ethicists
