# LIE — Logical Intuitive Extrovert (Napoleon / Jack)

**Socionics type:** LIE (ENTj)  
**Quadra:** Beta  
**MBTI equivalent:** ENTJ  
**Function Stack:** 1Te 2Ni 3Si 4Fe 5Ti 6Ne 7Se 8Fi

---

## Function Profile

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism, results |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Ni | Future projections, time, meaning |
| **3rd (Role)** | Si | Comfort, physical sensations |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Fe | Emotional expression, atmosphere |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Ti | Structural logic, consistency |
| **6th (Activation)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Se | Power, control |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Fi | Values, warmth |

---

## Characteristics

LIE (Napoleon) is the entrepreneur and organizer. Known for strategic thinking, ambition, and driving projects to completion.

### Strengths
- Strong organizational and management abilities
- Strategic vision for business and projects
- Decisive and action-oriented
- Good at motivating others toward goals

### Challenges
- May prioritize efficiency over people's feelings
- Weak ethics (Fe PoLR) means difficulty with emotional atmosphere
- Can be seen as demanding or controlling
- May struggle with work-life balance

### Famous Examples
- Napoleon Bonaparte
- Entrepreneurs and business leaders
