# ILI — Intuitive Logical Introvert (Balzac)

**Socionics type:** ILI (INTp)  
**Quadra:** Gamma  
**MBTI equivalent:** INTP  
**Function Stack:** 1Ni 2Te 3Fi 4Se 5Ne 6Si 7Fe 8Ti

---

## Function Profile

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Ni | Future projections, meaning, development |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism, results |
| **3rd (Role)** | Fi | Values, warmth, personal relations |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Se | Power, control, physical force |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas |
| **6th (Activation)** | Si | Comfort, physical sensations |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Fe | Emotional expression |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Ti | Structural logic |

---

## Characteristics

ILI (Balzac) is the analyst and critic. Known for strategic thinking, deep analysis, and long-term vision.

### Strengths
- Excellent strategic and analytical thinking
- Visionary about future developments
- Objective and critical perspective
- Good at identifying inefficiencies

### Challenges
- May appear pessimistic or critical
- Weak sensing (Se PoLR) means discomfort with physical reality
- Can be seen as overly demanding or perfectionist
- May neglect immediate practical matters

### Famous Examples
- Balzac (writer)
- Analysts, critics, strategic planners
