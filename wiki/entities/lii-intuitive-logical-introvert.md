# LII — Intuitive Logical Introvert (Robespierre)

**Socionics type:** LII (INTj)  
**Quadra:** Alpha  
**MBTI equivalent:** INTJ  
**Function Stack:** 1Ti 2Ne 3Se 4Fe 5Te 6Fi 7Ni 8Se

---

| Function | Element | Description |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **1st (Base)** | Ti | Structural logic, consistency, classification |
| **2nd (Creative)** | Ne | Possibilities, ideas, creativity |
| **3rd (Role)** | Se | Power, control, physical environment |
| **4th (PoLR)** | Fe | Emotional expression, atmosphere |
| **5th (Suggestive)** | Te | Efficiency, pragmatism |
| **6th (Activation)** | Fi | Values, warmth, relations |
| **7th (Ignoring)** | Ni | Future projections, time |
| **8th (Demonstrative)** | Se | Power, influence |

---

## Characteristics

LII (Robespierre) is the analyst and strategist. Known for their logical precision and strategic thinking.

### Strengths
- Exceptional analytical and logical capabilities
- Strategic vision for long-term planning
- Objective and fair-minded
- Deep knowledge in areas of interest

### Challenges
- May appear cold or distant emotionally
- Weak ethics function (Fe PoLR) means difficulty with emotional expression
- Can be overly critical of others' logic
- May struggle with physical/sensory aspects of life

### Famous Examples
- Robespierre (historical figure)
- Strategic analysts and system architects
