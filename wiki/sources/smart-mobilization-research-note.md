# Smart Mobilization Research Note

## What Already Exists

- Military recruitment research is a mature field.
- Ukraine already uses role-based recruitment channels, especially via public vacancy platforms.
- Current Ukrainian vacancy feeds already expose useful filters by branch and specialty, including IT, communications, UAV, EW/RER, logistics, medicine, engineering, reconnaissance, staff, and more.

## What We Did Not Find

- No clear public evidence of **socionics**, **psychosophy**, or **temporistics** being used as a standard military recruitment framework.
- No public Ukrainian military system was found that formally maps typology types to roles as an operational recruitment layer.

## Project Novelty

Our project combines:

- live role catalogs from Ukrainian recruitment sources
- typology-based fit hypotheses
- civil profession as a tie-breaker
- voluntary, non-coercive role recommendations

This makes the project an **experimental typology-based matching layer** on top of existing role-based recruitment.

## Practical Implication

The most defensible use case is not forced assignment, but **better voluntary placement** into a role family that matches:

- current military needs
- civil skills
- typological strengths
