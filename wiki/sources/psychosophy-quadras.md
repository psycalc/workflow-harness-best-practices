# Psychosophy Quadras

## Overview

In the project source set, Psychosophy is grouped into **6 quadras**, each containing 4 fixed types. The grouping used here follows the community source tradition summarized in the Psychosophy Library and related Russian materials.

The key idea is that a quadra is not defined by a single leading aspect alone, but by a **stable type cluster** and its dominant thematic orientation.

## Quadra Structure

| Quadra | Theme | Type Cluster |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| **1** | Survival / Cleaning | ЭЛВФ, ЭВЛФ, ФВЛЭ, ФЛВЭ |
| **2** | Agriculture / Household | ЭФВЛ, ЭВФЛ, ЛВФЭ, ЛФВЭ |
| **3** | Centralized Management | ЭЛФВ, ЭФЛВ, ВФЛЭ, ВЛФЭ |
| **4** | Culture / Development | ЛЭФВ, ЛФЭВ, ВФЭЛ, ВЭФЛ |
| **5** | Consumption | ВЛЭФ, ВЭЛФ, ФЛЭВ, ФЭЛВ |
| **6** | Reorganization | ФВЭЛ, ФЭВЛ, ЛЭВФ, ЛВЭФ |

## Quadra Descriptions

### 1. Survival / Cleaning

**Core orientation:** `В + Л` as process, `Ф + Э` as result.

This quadra centers on inner backbone, self-definition, and direct structuring of reality. It tends to value clear priorities, controlled effort, and the ability to reduce disorder quickly.

**Typical strengths:**
- decisive self-organization
- clarity under pressure
- practical filtering of unnecessary complexity

**Typical blind spots:**
- weak tolerance for diffuse emotional or material drift
- impatience with unstructured dependence

### 2. Agriculture / Household

**Core orientation:** `В + Ф` as process, `Л + Э` as result.

This quadra emphasizes stewardship, maintenance, and the practical management of resources, routines, and obligations. It is oriented toward stable everyday functioning and workable support structures.

**Typical strengths:**
- resource handling
- household/order maintenance
- steady practical support

**Typical blind spots:**
- excessive focus on duty or comfort at the expense of abstract system-building

### 3. Centralized Management

**Core orientation:** `Ф + Л` as process, `В + Э` as result.

This quadra is strongly associated with organizing systems, central coordination, and formalized control. The emphasis is on efficiency, hierarchy, and making structure serve action.

**Typical strengths:**
- administration and coordination
- formal planning
- operational discipline

**Typical blind spots:**
- pressure to control outcomes too rigidly
- reduced flexibility in emotional or interpersonal matters

### 4. Culture / Development

**Core orientation:** `Э + Ф` as process, `В + Л` as result.

This quadra prioritizes atmosphere, refinement, and the human side of practical life. It tends to value supportive environments, cultural development, and the combination of emotional tone with material stability.

**Typical strengths:**
- relational sensitivity
- social support
- maintenance of humane environment

**Typical blind spots:**
- over-investment in atmosphere versus hard structure

### 5. Consumption

**Core orientation:** `Э + Л` as process, `Ф + В` as result.

This quadra is oriented toward intellectual-emotional exchange, interpretation, evaluation, and the active use of ideas in social life. It often looks like a quadra of critique, discussion, and selective consumption of information and meaning.

**Typical strengths:**
- evaluation and judgment
- concept-driven communication
- social and intellectual selectivity

**Typical blind spots:**
- over-analysis or over-selection
- drifting away from concrete implementation

### 6. Reorganization

**Core orientation:** `В + Э` as process, `Ф + Л` as result.

This quadra is associated with restructuring, renewal, and mobilizing people through will and emotional charge. It tends to deal well with transitions, reorganization, and situations where an old order must be replaced.

**Typical strengths:**
- restructuring systems
- rallying people around change
- mobilization in unstable conditions

**Typical blind spots:**
- tension between intensity and practical consolidation

## Practical Use

For applied typing in this project, quadras are most useful as **broad role-fit filters**:

- they show what kind of environment feels natural
- they show what kind of work is energizing vs draining
- they are not enough alone to select a precise job or military specialty
